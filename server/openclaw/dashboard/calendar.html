Mission
Caly — the single source of truth for time. Passive calendar layer that shows what's happening and answers questions about schedule.
Role in the system
Fetches and displays events from Google Calendar. Shows the current week at a glance. Answers scheduling questions on request. Does not push notifications or modify events.
Why it matters
Travel Agent, School Agent, and CoS all need to reason about time. Without a calendar layer that owns scheduling, conflicts happen and Q2 work never gets protected.
Connects to
Used by CoS (weekly planning), Travel Agent (conflict detection), School Agent (pickup reminders). Reads from Google Calendar via local MCP.
Does NOT overlap with
Caly doesn't know why you're going to Stadtlohn — just that the slot is taken. Context lives in Travel Agent. Caly is read-only: it never creates or modifies events.
